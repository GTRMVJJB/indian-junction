Our Story

Indian Spice.
Dutch Heart.

"A culinary haven where cultures unite, spices dance, and every dish is a tribute to India."

Founded by Priya, Aman, and Simran, Indian Junction was born from a shared dream: to bring the warmth of Indian hospitality to the Netherlands. Embracing Dutch life while staying rooted in tradition, they crafted a space where every meal is made with care, using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients.

As proud sponsors of a local Uithoorn hockey team, their warmth extends well beyond the kitchen. Celebrated by de Volkskrant and critic Hiske Versprille, the restaurant has become a beloved destination for Indian cuisine in the region.
